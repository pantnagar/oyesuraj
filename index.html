About Suraj

The Man Behind the Laughs

Hey everyone, I'm Suraj! I started this channel back in 2017 as a fun project to share my love for gaming and cricket. It's been an amazing journey, and I'm so grateful for every single one of you who has joined me along the way.

We hit our first major milestone of 1,000 subscribers in 2021, and it's all thanks to your incredible support. My goal is to keep creating content that makes you laugh, and to build a community where everyone feels welcome.

When I'm not making videos, you can find me playing with my pets, exploring new places, or just chilling out with friends and family. Thanks for being a part of the OYEsuraj family!
